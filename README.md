<h1>Pioneer Receiver AutoConnect</h1>

<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Pioneer Receiver AutoConnect is an Android app designed to automatically connect to any Pioneer receiver with networking capabilities. The app supports most popular inputs and allows for custom commands based on your receiver's user manual. It includes Video/Audio menus as well as Surround menus, with arrows and a select button to navigate them.</p>

<h2>Features</h2>
<ul>
    <li><strong>AutoConnect:</strong> Automatically connects to Pioneer receivers on the local network.</li>
    <li><strong>Popular Inputs:</strong> Access to most popular input options.</li>
    <li><strong>Custom Commands:</strong> Input custom commands using the receiver's user manual.</li>
    <li><strong>Menus:</strong> Navigate Video/Audio and Surround menus with directional arrows and a select button.</li>
    <li><strong>Remote Connection:</strong> Connect through the internet by forwarding port 8102 to your receiver.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Requirements</h2>
<ul>
    <li>Pioneer receiver with networking capabilities.</li>
    <li>Android device.</li>
    <li>Local network for AutoConnect functionality.</li>
    <li>Port 8102 forwarded to your receiver for remote connection.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Installation</h2>
<ol>
    <li>Download and install the app from the <a href="https://github.com/oibarra93/PioneerRemote-kotlin/releases">Releases Page</a>.</li>
    <li>Ensure your Android device is connected to the same local network as your Pioneer receiver.</li>
    <li>Open the app and it will automatically connect to your receiver.</li>
</ol>

<h2>Usage</h2>
<ul>
    <li><strong>Local Network Connection:</strong> Ensure your Android device and Pioneer receiver are on the same local network. The app will auto-connect.</li>
    <li><strong>Remote Connection:</strong> Forward port 8102 to your receiver and use the app to connect over the internet.</li>
</ul>
